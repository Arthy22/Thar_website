How would you describe Thar

The Mahindra Thar is an off road beast that marries rugged charm with practicality. As a user, I m thrilled with its robust build and go anywhere attitude. The convertible top adds a fun element, perfect for sunny drives. The interior, though not luxurious, exudes a utilitarian vibe. The engine s power on challenging terrains is commendable. However, city driving reveals a bit of a bumpy ride, and the steering could be more responsive. Overall, for adventure seekers who crave off road prowess, the Thar is a thrilling companion, but for daily urban use, some compromises are inevitable.

How would you describe Thar

The Mahindra Thar is a legendary off roader reborn in a new generation. It has an authentic exterior design and comes with a hard top or soft top roof. Power comes from a 2 liter turbo diesel engine that produces 130 horsepower. This gives the Thar good performance both on and off the road. While ride quality is stiff for highway usage, the Thar excels on rough terrains thanks to its capable 4x4 drivetrain. It gets basic features but offers premium off road capabilities. Safety and comfort features are basic due to its purpose built nature.
